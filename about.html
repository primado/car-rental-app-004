About US

Deliver the exclusive transportation experience you’re accustomed

Made by Car Enthusiasts for Car Enthusiasts

Falcon Car Rental was founded in 2015 by a team of car enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for luxury and exotic cars. Each day, we strive to exceed customer expectations with access to the latest models, along with top-of-the-line customer experience. We’re proud to be the premier luxury and exotic car rental service in Los Angeles, and we invite you to enjoy the legendary craftsmanship and meticulous engineering featured in our collection.

State-of-the-Art Vehicles

We have an elite collection of the best sports cars, luxury SUVs, and exotic vehicles for you to choose from. Even our most distinguished clients are impressed with our fleet of internationally recognized brands, including Bentley, Rolls-Royce, Tesla, Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Maserati, Porsche, Range Rover, Mercedes, BMW, and Cadillac.

Exclusive Driving Experience

Each of our vehicles is equipped with the most technologically advanced features and customized interiors to allow you access to the best the car world has to offer. Choose from cars that blend eco-friendly emissions and state-of-the-art design, or combine classic lines with precise control. From the luxe aesthetics of the Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead to the roaring power of the Lamborghini Huracan Spyder 610-4 to the technological prowess of the Tesla Model S, our entire fleet represents the height of vehicular innovation.

World-Class Service

We are dedicated to meeting your every need, so you can enjoy every moment. Our collection of exotic supercars is the perfect marriage of the highest-class engineering and superior style. Sit back and relax in the spacious and powerfully sophisticated Cadillac Escalade ESV or take the wheel with the speed, power, and control of a McLaren 675LT.
